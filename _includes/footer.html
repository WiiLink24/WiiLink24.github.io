About WiiLink

WiiLink24 is a revival service for the now-defunct Japanese exclusive Wii channels. This service allows you to use Wii no Ma and the Digicam Print Channel. Soon it will have the Demae Channel available!

2020-2021 WiiLink Team

We are not associated with Nintendo nor its affiliates. We are also not associated with other services for the Wii.
