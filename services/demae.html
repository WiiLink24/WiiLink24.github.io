Demae

To your house without moving a finger.

Deliver your favorites directly from your Wii.

Order anything available.

Secure.

The Demae Channel was a Japan-exclusive Channel that allowed you to deliver food to your home (sushi, chicken, pizza, hamburgers, etc). The service was provided by Demae-Can. It was later released for the Wii U, but it was discontinued on March 31, 2017 for both the Wii and the Wii U.
